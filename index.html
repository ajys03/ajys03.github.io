Project Experience

Identity Matching Using Machine Learning (IMUML)

Data Analyst | May 2023 - Aug 2023

  • During my internship at the University of Michigan ITS, I took part in project IMUML. The purpose of the project was to evaluate and recommend the most appropriate AI development framework to match identities. We first began reading and analyzing multiple research papers on the topic of identity matching with machine learning. After 3 weeks of research, we came across the Hierarchical Naive Bayes (HNB) model: an improved version of the Naïve Bayes classifier. As a data analyst, in order to replicate the HNB model, I conducted EDA on ~600,000 records using Python, preprocessed ~600,000 records in accordance with data privary regulations, and helped build the unsupervised model. Overall, it was an amazing experience which allowed me to dip my toes into machine learning.
